Closed Roll Year
The year listed represents the leading year of the fiscal year that the assessed value is effective for taxation purposes. For example, 2009 represents the 2009-2010 fiscal year during which the assessed values listed were effective (i.e. billed and collected). Note that a fiscal year starts July 1st and ends June 30th so that 2009 would start on July 1st, 2009 and end on June 30th 2010.
